She Moved an 800 lb Log.
With One Tool.

No machines. No crew. Just a chunk of firewood, a LogOX, and a little physics.

One of our customers needed to reposition a 700 to 800 lb locust log. Instead of calling in heavy equipment, she placed a chunk of firewood as a fulcrum, used her LogOX 3-in-1 as a lever, and moved it in seconds.

How People Actually Use Theirs

The 3-in-1 was built for firewood. But our customers keep finding new ways to put it to work.

Elevate and Cut

Lift logs off the ground with the Timberjack so your chainsaw never touches dirt. Cleaner cuts, longer chain life, less time sharpening.

Roll and Position

Use the 40" Cant Hook to roll heavy logs exactly where you need them. Load trailers, feed splitters, or reposition timber without a second pair of hands.

Grab and Go

The 21" Hauler hooks into rounds so you can grip, lift, and carry without ever bending over. Your back does zero work. That's the point.
