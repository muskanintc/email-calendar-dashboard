How It Works

One Pass Down the Log. Every Cut Marked.

Click a marking wheel onto the mainframe. Load the paint canister on top. Roll it down the length of the log.

The wheel lays a clean paint mark at every interval. Cut right on the line. Uniform lengths, every time, without bending over or breaking out a tape measure.

The Mingo Marker includes:

  • Mingo Marker mainframe unit
  • One marking wheel
  • Paint canister mount, ready to roll out of the box
