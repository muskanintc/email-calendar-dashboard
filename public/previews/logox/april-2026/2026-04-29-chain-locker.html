Why It Matters

A Sharp Chain Is Useless If You Cannot Find It.

Most chains do not die on the bar. They die in a tangled pile at the bottom of a tote. Rusted, dulled against each other, or lost.

The Chain Locker gives every chain its own slot. Stored flat, labeled, and ready for the next cut.
