Turn Your Existing Chainsaw
Into a Portable Sawmill

If you already own a chainsaw, you're one attachment away from milling your own boards, slabs, beams, and posts from any log, anywhere you need it.

The Granberg Alaskan Mill clamps directly to your chainsaw bar and turns it into a precision milling system. No stationary sawmill. No hauling logs to a shop. We put together four bundles so you get everything you need in one box.

Which Bundle Fits Your Setup?

Every bundle matches a chainsaw bar size. Pick the one that fits your saw, and you're ready to mill. Not sure? Start with the bar length printed on your chainsaw.

The Bundles

Best for: Firewood and Small Projects

Homeowner Bundle 20"

Built for homeowners who want to mill firewood rounds, small beams, and planks. Clamps to any chainsaw with a 20" bar or shorter. Lightweight, compact, and easy to set up in your yard or at the woodlot.

  • Cuts slabs up to 18 inches wide
  • Cutting depth: 1/2" to 13"
  • Fits chainsaws with 20" bar, 50cc+ engine
  • Chainsaw bar must be solid steel (not laminate)
  • Includes Slabbing Rail Bracket Set (G850) — brackets that attach to 2x4s and secure to the log, giving your mill a level guide for a perfectly flat first cut
  • Includes Precision Chain Grinder (G1012XT) — 12V grinder that sharpens ripping chains in the field so your cuts stay clean and efficient

Best for: General Milling

Backcountry Bundle 24"

The starting point for serious milling. The MK-IV frame is built heavier than the Homeowner model and includes the G555B Edging Mill, so you can square up logs before slabbing. Great for cutting dimensional lumber from smaller hardwoods and softwoods.

  • Cuts slabs up to 20 inches wide
  • Cutting depth: 1/2" to 13"
  • Fits chainsaws with 24" bar, 50cc+ engine
  • Includes Edging Mill (G555B) — a vertical milling attachment that squares logs and cuts dimensional lumber without rolling the log
  • Includes Slabbing Rail Bracket Set (G850) and Precision Chain Grinder (G1012XT)
