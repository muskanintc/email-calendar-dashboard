Split a Full Cord.
Never Hunch Over.

Last stage, we talked about moving the rounds. This stage is about splitting them.

If you have ever spent a full day muscling heavy rounds onto a splitter, you already know where the strain comes from. It is not the splitting. It is the loading. Lifting 50, 100, 150 pound rounds from the ground up to deck height, over and over. That is the part that wears you down.

The Lift

Dan-D-Lift Manual Log Lift

Load heavy rounds without the heavy lifting.

The Dan-D-Lift is a manual log lift attachment built for the TimberOX Summit. Roll a round onto the 20" platform at ground level. Use the 52" handle to lever it up to splitting height. A ratcheting safety panel locks the arm in place so you can pause and reposition at any point.

Handles logs up to roughly 175 pounds. Installs with two pins and two bolts. No special tools required.
