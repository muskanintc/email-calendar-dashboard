What Is Sonar Send?

Sonar Send is a tool that sits on top of your existing Klaviyo flows and adds extra touchpoints (email, SMS, or both) to people who didn't convert from the original Klaviyo flow message.

How it works: Customer triggers a Klaviyo flow (e.g., abandons checkout) → Klaviyo sends its normal flow email → If that person doesn't convert, Sonar Send fires an additional message to try to recover the sale → Triple Whale pixel tracks the attribution.

It's not replacing your Klaviyo emails. It's a second-chance layer that re-engages non-converters with an additional send.

Revenue Consistency

Key observation: Klaviyo abandonment flows show large revenue spikes ($4K–$7K) on certain days. Sonar revenue appears as tiny dots at the bottom of the chart, barely visible in comparison. Sonar's revenue also spikes on random individual days and drops to $0 on most days.

With only 6 total purchases in 30 days, one large order on one random day can dramatically swing the numbers. This is not a steady revenue stream yet. It's a handful of recovered orders scattered across the month.

6

What "Expanding" Actually Means

There are two different things expansion could refer to:

Option A: Complete the existing setup (must do first) Turn on the Abandoned Cart flow. It's still in Draft generating $0. This is a medium-high intent flow sitting between Browse and Checkout. This isn't "expanding." This is finishing the basic setup that should already be live.
Option B: Add Sonar to entirely new flow types This means adding Sonar touchpoints to welcome flows, post-purchase, winback, etc. This is true expansion. But you cannot evaluate whether expansion makes sense when 1 of 3 existing flows isn't even active. You need all 3 flows running for at least 30 days to understand Sonar's real ceiling on abandonment before going broader.
7

Recommendation

Before expanding Sonar to new flow types, complete and evaluate the existing setup:

1
Activate the Abandoned Cart flow immediately It's the highest-volume abandonment flow sitting at $0. This is the single biggest quick win available right now.
2
Run all 3 flows for a full 30 days Get complete data on the entire abandonment funnel with Sonar active. Current data only reflects 2 of 3 flows.
3
Evaluate ROI after 30 days If all 3 flows together generate $2,000+/month and the return vs Sonar's cost makes sense, expand to other flow types. If not, you'll know the ceiling.
Key question to resolve: What is the monthly cost for Sonar Send? The $1,206/month needs to be weighed against the cost. If Sonar costs $200/month, the ROI is strong. If it's $800/month, we need all 3 flows performing before it makes financial sense.
