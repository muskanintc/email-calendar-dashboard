3 TIPS FOR A CONFIDENT SUMMER

01

Pack a spare pair for every travel day.

Rest stops are unpredictable. A spare pair in your bag means you never have to cut a trip short or rush to find a restroom. Roll it up, toss it in your bag. You'll forget it's there until you need it.

02

Match your absorbency to your activity.

A quick walk to the mailbox and a full day at a family reunion are not the same. Regular works for light days at home. Super or Maximum gives you the coverage for longer outings when a restroom isn't always close.

03

Skip the drugstore hunt. Bring reusable.

Traveling with disposables means packing a whole supply or hoping you can find your brand at an unfamiliar store. Wearever packs like regular underwear, washes in any hotel sink or laundromat, and is ready to wear again. No restocking needed.
