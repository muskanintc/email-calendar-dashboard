AN HONEST GUIDE

Is Your Absorbency Level
Actually Working?

Most people stick with the absorbency level they started with. But your needs change. If any of these sound familiar, it might be time to step up.

4 SIGNS IT'S TIME TO UPGRADE

☑   You're changing more than once or twice a day.

If you're going through multiple pairs before the day is over, your current level isn't absorbing enough. That's not normal wear. That's the wrong fit for your needs.

☑   You feel damp before you reach a restroom.

Protection should keep you dry between restroom visits. If you're feeling moisture before you get there, you need more absorbency capacity working for you.

☑   You're skipping outings because you're not confident.

Saying no to lunch, a walk, or a trip to the store because you're worried about leaks? That's your protection failing you. The right absorbency level gives you your day back.

☑   You're doubling up with pads or liners.

If you're layering disposable pads inside your incontinence underwear for backup, you're compensating for the wrong absorbency level. One pair should be enough. With the right level, it is.
