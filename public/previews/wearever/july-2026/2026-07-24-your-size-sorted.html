Find Your Fit

Your Size, Sorted

The right size is what makes Wearever feel like your own underwear, comfortable, secure, and completely reliable. Here is how to land on your perfect fit the first time.

Three Simple Steps

1

Measure your waist and hips

Use a soft tape measure around the fullest part. Two quick numbers are all you need.
2

Check the size chart on the product page

Every Wearever style lists its measurements. Match your numbers to the chart for that style.
3

Between sizes? Go up

A slightly roomier fit stays comfortable all day and keeps the protection right where it belongs.

A Good Fit Should Feel Like Nothing at All

Snug but never tight, smooth under clothes, and soft against your skin. When the size is right, you forget you are even wearing it.
