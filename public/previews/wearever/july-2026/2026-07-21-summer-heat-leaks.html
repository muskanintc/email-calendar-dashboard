Summer and Your Bladder

Why Summer Heat Makes Leaks Worse

If you feel like leaks show up more often in July, you are not imagining it. Heat changes how much you drink and how your bladder behaves. Here is what is going on, and how to stay ahead of it.

Three Reasons the Heat Works Against You

1

You drink more in the heat

Staying hydrated is exactly right, but more fluids also mean more trips and more sudden urges.
2

Summer favorites do not help

Iced coffee, soda, and a cold beer are all bladder irritants that can turn up the urge to go.
3

You are out and about more

Long days outdoors mean fewer easy restroom stops and more chances for a leak to catch you off guard.

How to Stay a Step Ahead

✓  Keep sipping water. Cutting back to avoid leaks actually concentrates urine and irritates the bladder more.

✓  Go easy on caffeine and alcohol at the cookout.

✓  Take a bathroom break before you head out and again when you arrive.

✓  Wear protection that is breathable and comfortable in the heat, so you can simply stop thinking about it.
