Notes for Designer

  • Overall Style Reference: Melissa & Doug emails (Sizzlin' Summer Savings, A Puzzlingly Good Deal). Bright, playful, product-focused with strong offer placement.
  • Brand Colors: Primary Green #20AB5D (CTAs, trust bar), Yellow #F0C419 (accents, offer highlights), Blue #3F8DC7 (secondary buttons, headings), White #FFFFFF (backgrounds)
  • Font: Nunito (bold for headings, regular for body)
  • CTA Buttons: Green (#20AB5D), fully rounded (50px radius), white bold text, generous padding
  • Product Images: All images sourced from the product pages on artcreativity.com. Use the main hero image for each product (links provided in design notes above each card).
  • Tiered Offer Display: This is the most important visual element. Make it impossible to miss. Consider making the $300/20% tier physically larger or using a "BEST VALUE" badge on it.
  • Mobile Optimization: Email should be designed mobile-first. Single column layout. Large tap targets for CTAs. Product images should be full-width on mobile.
  • No Pricing in Body: Do not show product prices in the email. The tiered discount is the offer. Let the site handle pricing.
  • No Hyphens or Dashes: Copy has been written without hyphens or dashes per brand guidelines.
  • Summer Theme: Consider adding subtle summer-themed illustrations (sun, bubbles, confetti) to the hero and section dividers to reinforce the seasonal vibe.
  • Emoji Note: Emojis in the wireframe are placeholders for icons. Designer should replace with brand-appropriate icons or illustrated equivalents.
