BY COPS, FOR COPS

Our owners founded Zero9 because they were full time police officers who got tired of being issued equipment without a legitimate method to carry it on the job. Every piece of gear is custom designed with a minimalist footprint, to take advantage of the limited space you have on your vest and belt.

All our gear is made in the USA, by Cops for Cops. We build solutions that work, and every piece is Built to Win the Fight®.
