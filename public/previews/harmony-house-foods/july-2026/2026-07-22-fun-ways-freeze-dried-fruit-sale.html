10 Fun Ways to Use Freeze-Dried Fruit

Most people picture a quick snack or an oatmeal topping. Your fruit can do far more. Here are two favorites to start.

Way 1

Blend It Into Fruit Powders

Pulse freeze-dried fruit into a fine powder to flavor yogurt, frosting, pancake batter, and smoothie bowls. Blueberry and strawberry powders double as natural food coloring.

Way 2

Upgrade Your Charcuterie Board

Freeze-dried fruit adds color and crunch to a board. Try cherries with aged cheddar, mango with a spicy cheese, or strawberries alongside dark chocolate.
