#1

It is IP68 Rated.

Full submersion at 1.5 meters for 30 minutes. Rain, puddles, full downpour. The TRIAD PRO stays online when everything else quits.

 

#2

Detach the Mic. Not the Radio.

The TRIAD PRO uses a 6-pin quick disconnect system. The mic detaches from the radio connection without unplugging the entire setup. Your cable stays routed through your plate carrier, MOLLE, or uniform. Swap accessories, replace a damaged mic, or disconnect at end of shift. No re-routing. Less wear on your radio.

 

#3

It Works With Your Earpiece.

Built in 3.5mm audio port. Plug in your AWARE, your HDLO, or any listen-only earpiece you already run. No adapters. No workarounds. It just connects.

 

#4

Straight Cord. Clean Routing.

20 inches. Straight. No coil to snag on your carrier or catch during movement. Routes through plate carriers, external vests, and duty belts without adding bulk. Clip it, route it, run it.

 

#5

10 Radio Platforms. One PTT.

Motorola APX, NEXT, XTS, XPR. Harris XL and XG series. Kenwood NX/TK and 2-pin. Tait TP. Whatever your department runs, the TRIAD PRO has an adapter for it.

NOTES FOR DESIGNER

  • Match the editorial quality and lifestyle image treatment from the March TRIAD PRO email that performed well. Dark, tactical, field-authentic.
  • Hero image (officer portrait) and action image (hand on PTT) are full-width, edge to edge. These are the strongest shots.
  • Product detail images (front face, QD system, back clip) are centered with padding, max 320-440px. They serve as supporting visuals, not heroes.
  • Each "Did You Know" fact is a numbered section. The red #1 through #5 numbers create a scannable rhythm down the email.
  • Radio compatibility grid: 2x2 dark cards (#1e1e1e) on charcoal (#282828). Red brand names, grey model numbers.
  • FAQ section: Stacked dark cards with white question, grey answer. Clean, scannable, no accordion needed in email.
  • All CTAs: Sheepdog Red (#DE2A2A), white text, Archivo Black, tracked uppercase.
  • Mobile-first: 600px max. All images scale. FAQ cards and radio grid maintain padding on mobile.
  • No white backgrounds on any product images. If the .png product shots have white backgrounds, composite them onto dark/tactical backgrounds.
