WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

Built With a Thin Wire

The AWARE series takes a different approach to earpiece design. Built with a thin wire that carries audio directly, so no tube is required.

The wire is shaped to follow the natural contour of the ear. It sits in place on its own, clean and low profile.

For operators looking for a streamlined, low maintenance setup, this is built for that.

Stays Where You Put It

The thin wire is shaped to follow the contour of your ear, so it sits naturally and stays put through movement, transitions, and long shifts. Secure fit from start to end.

Wear it left ear or right. With the retention hook or without. Your call.

Three Eartips. One Earpiece.

Every AWARE earpiece ships with three eartip options so you can match your environment.

Medium Open Eartip

Standard fit. Maintains situational awareness. Hear your surroundings while staying connected.

Large Open Eartip

Secure hold for larger ear canals. Same open design for ambient sound.

High Noise Eartip

Isolates audio in loud environments. Range days. Heavy traffic. Crowds.
