Did You Know

It Runs NEXUS And 3.5mm At The Same Time.

One NEXUS (7.1mm) port for the tactical headset. One 3.5mm listen only port for a discreet earpiece. Transition from a standard setup to a full tactical headset without swapping mics.

02

Did You Know

There Are Four Buttons, Not One.

Dual PTT buttons, side and front facing, so you can key up from the position that matches your kit. Plus a dedicated emergency button for instant alert, a programmable button for whatever your unit needs it to do, and volume control on the mic.

It Is Rated IP67.

Full dust protection. Submersion protection. The ENGAGE is built to keep going through rain, mud, and the kind of weather that ends other mics.

It Speaks The Same Language As Your Headset.

Plug and play with NATO wired tactical headsets, including Ops-Core AMP, OTTO NoizeBarrier TAC, and 3M PELTOR Comtac models. One mic, the headsets your unit already runs.
