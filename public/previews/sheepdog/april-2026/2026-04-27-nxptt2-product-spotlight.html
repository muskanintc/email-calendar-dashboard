Dual-Purpose By Design

One PTT. Standard or Tactical.

The SHEEPDOG NXPTT2 is a dual-purpose Tactical PTT with a NEXUS TP-120/U174 connector and Remote Speaker Microphone built into one unit. Designed for use with tactical headsets.

Wired for NEXUS (NATO-US) and built so an operator can transition from standard to tactical operations without changing PTTs.

Feature 01

Two PTT Buttons

Front and side/top PTT placement. Keep a hand on the weapon, the radio, or the carrier. Press the one that fits the moment.

Feature 02

Audio Routes to the Headset

Plug a tactical headset into the NEXUS port and receive audio routes straight to the headset. Amplified for use with dynamic (low impedance) headsets.

Single-comms PTT and speaker microphone system in one rugged build.
