Frequently Asked Questions.

The TRIAD PRO is one of the most asked-about PTTs we carry. Here are the questions operators ask most, answered straight.

Shop TRIAD PRO
 

Q.

What is the cord length and overall size?

The TRIAD PRO features a 20-inch straight cord for clean routing through gear. The PTT itself measures approximately 3" x 2.4" x 1" and weighs 5 oz. Mid-sized. Larger than the original TRIAD series, but still compact enough to mount flat on your carrier without adding bulk.

Standalone TRIAD PRO with full 20-inch cord visible, showing the QD connector at the end. White background — composite onto dark charcoal. This image answers the "size" question visually.
 

Q.

Is the TRIAD PRO waterproof?

Yes. The TRIAD PRO is IP68 rated. Fully protected against dust and can be submerged in water up to 1.5 meters for 30 minutes without damage. Rain, sweat, puddles, full downpour. It stays operational.

 

Q.

Does the TRIAD PRO require an earpiece to work?

No. The TRIAD PRO has a built-in speaker and microphone. It works as a standalone unit right out of the box. But it also includes a 3.5mm earpiece port, so you can plug in a listen-only earpiece when you need discreet audio. Your choice.

Q.

How durable is the TRIAD PRO for field use?

Built for rugged environments. Reinforced housing, strain relief cabling, and a secure alligator clip that attaches to uniform shirts, plate carriers, or MOLLE systems. Operating temperature range: -4°F to 122°F. Designed for law enforcement, military, and tactical professionals who need gear that holds up shift after shift.

Q.

What earpieces work with the TRIAD PRO?

The TRIAD PRO has a standard 3.5mm earpiece port. It works with most 3.5mm listen-only earpieces, including Sheepdog AWARE and N-ear 360 Flexo. Available as a complete kit with an acoustic tube earpiece, or as a bundle with the N-ear Flexo.

 

Quick Disconnect

Includes a 6-Pin Quick Disconnect (QD) radio adapter for simple setup and removal, using our simple "push-pull" design. QD is ideal for those who prioritize speed and efficiency.
