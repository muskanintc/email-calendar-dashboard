The Chain Breaker Collection captures the moment a man decides to rise above what once held him back. Each pendant showcases a powerful animal spirit shattering its restraints. Symbols of victory, mastery, and purpose. These pieces aren't just jewelry. They're declarations of strength and legacy.

Every piece in this collection carries over 36 diamonds around the border, with gemstone eyes that are unique to each animal. Flip it over and you'll find "VINCULA FRANGE" engraved on the back. Latin for "Break the Chains." And on every single reverse, a chain is being shattered by a weapon that ties back to the animal's meaning.

Five animals. Five stories. Both sides tell a story. Available in silver and gold.
