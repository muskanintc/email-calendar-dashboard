Behind the Piece

Watch the Story of the Stoic Pendant

Watch on YouTube

I did not set out to make jewelry. I set out to make something a man could look down at on a hard day and remember who he wants to be.

The Stoic pendant is built around the four virtues the old philosophers actually lived by. Wisdom. Courage. Justice. Temperance. A compass rose holds them together at the center, because a good life needs direction, not just good intentions.

We filmed the whole thing, from the first sketch to the final pour. Watch it above, then turn the piece over and read the line we put on the back.
