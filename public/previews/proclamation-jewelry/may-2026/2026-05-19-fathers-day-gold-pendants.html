NOTES FOR DESIGNER

  • Layout inspired by David Yurman "8 Forever Favorites" email. Numbered zigzag: odd products have image left / text right, even products have text left / image right. Creates visual rhythm as you scroll.
  • Numbers: Very large (96px+), italic, Trajan Pro or similar serif. Gold (#D4AF37). Should be the dominant visual element in each row alongside the product image.
  • Product images: All should be on dark backgrounds. If originals have white/light backgrounds, composite onto dark (#111) or use dark variant shots from product pages. Each product has 3+ images on Shopify.
  • Product rows: Subtle 4px gap between rows. Dark card backgrounds (#111111). No borders.
  • Mobile: At 600px breakpoint, image stacks on top (full width), text stacks below (centered). Numbers scale down to ~72px.
  • The zigzag structure is the key design element. It's what makes this email feel editorial and curated vs. a product grid.
  • No pricing shown anywhere. SHOP NOW as underlined text links, not buttons (within the product rows). Gold buttons only for the main CTAs at top and bottom.
  • Typography: Trajan Pro for numbers, product names, and headings. Optima/Palatino for body and descriptions.
