The Story

His heart exposed. Crowned with thorns. Burning with a love so fierce it outlasted the cross itself.

The Sacred Heart is sacrifice, mercy, and unwavering devotion. Christ as both Savior and King, offering redemption through suffering and strength through faith. The surrounding rays of light represent the eternal truth that even in darkness, His love endures.

Every element of this creation carries meaning. The exposed heart is not vulnerability. It is an invitation. The thorns are not punishment. They are proof. And the flame that rises from the center is the love that could not be extinguished, not by betrayal, not by crucifixion, not by death.

This is devotion you can hold in your hand. Faith with weight. A reminder that the greatest act of strength the world has ever witnessed was an act of love.

"One of the soldiers pierced His side with a spear, and at once there came out blood and water."
