The Wolf

The most disciplined leader in the wild walks alone first. Then leads the rest of the pack.

The wolf is not the loudest creature in the wild. Not the largest. Not the first to move.

What it carries is something different. Patience. Loyalty. Quiet command. The kind of authority that does not need to announce itself.

Where other predators rely on force, the wolf relies on intention. Every step measured. Every alliance earned. Every fight chosen.

A Note on April

The Month of Transition

Winter does not end at once. Spring does not arrive in a single day. April lives in the space between, where the discipline to keep moving matters more than the conditions to move through.

It is the wolf's month.
