YOUR CHAIN

The sweet spot is mid-chest or just below your collarbone.

At this length, your chain rests naturally against your chest. It peeks through an open collar without competing. It sits clean under a crew neck without bunching.

When the length is right, you forget it is there. It becomes part of you. That is the point.

YOUR BRACELET

The perfect bracelet fit gives you slight movement. That is it.

It sits on the wrist bone and settles back into place on its own. One finger of room between the bracelet and your skin. That is the range.

At this fit, it moves with you, not against you. Comfortable all day. Looks intentional every time.

ONE MORE THING

Different necklines call for different chain lengths. What works with a crew neck will sit differently with an open collar.

Try a few lengths and find what feels natural on you. Once you know your fit, every piece you own looks more cohesive, more intentional, more you.
